  • Speech Recognition

Speech Recognition is a technology that allows a computer or machine to understand and process human speech. It converts spoken words into text or commands using Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP).

Examples:

Virtual assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant use speech recognition to take voice commands.

It’s also used in voice typing, call centers, and smart devices.

Key Point: Speech recognition helps in creating hands-free and user-friendly systems that understand natural human language.

  1. Product Recommendations

Product Recommendation is a Machine Learning application that helps systems suggest products to users based on their interests, behavior, and past activities.

Examples:

Amazon recommends products similar to what you’ve bought or viewed.

Netflix and YouTube recommend shows or videos based on your watch history.

Key Point: It improves user experience and increases sales or engagement by showing personalized suggestions to every user.
